Clustering Models

K-Means Clustering

K-Means Clustering is a partitioning method that divides the dataset into K distinct, non-overlapping subgroups or clusters.

Hierarchical Clustering

Hierarchical Clustering builds a multilevel hierarchy of clusters by creating a cluster tree, also known as a dendrogram.

DBSCAN

DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise) is a clustering method that identifies clusters and noise in a dataset.
